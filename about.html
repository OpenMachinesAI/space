Our Mission

The Central Space Program (CSP) aims to inspire students in STEM through hands-on rocket engineering, fostering innovation in propulsion, reuse, and autonomous flight systems.

What We Do

We design, build, and launch multi-stage rockets using electric ducted fans and solid motors, develop flight computers with micro:bit guidance, and share findings with the community.
